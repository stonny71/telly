padding

usage:

Padding classes are 8 in number

  • padding gives 8px Padding to an element
  • padding-top gives 8px top padding to an element
  • padding-bottom gives 8px bottom padding to an element
  • padding-left gives 8px left padding to an element
  • padding-right gives 8px right padding to an element
  • padding-small gives 8px padding to an element
  • padding-large gives top and bottom padding of 16px with left and right Padding of 8px
  • padding-larger gives top and bottom padding of 32px with left and right Padding of 8px
  • padding-none sets the padding to 0px

usage:
